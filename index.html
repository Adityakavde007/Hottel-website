About Us

Welcome to the Hotel Yaro Da Katta, where we serve up hearty, nutritious, and budget-friendly meals every day. Our goal is to provide students with a wide variety of fresh, wholesome food options to fuel both body and mind. Located conveniently within the campus, our mess offers a variety of meals that cater to different tastes and dietary needs, from traditional favorites to more contemporary dishes. Whether you’re in the mood for a quick snack, a full meal, or something in between, we’ve got you covered. We take pride in offering balanced meals that are not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients to keep you energized throughout the day. we believe in creating a warm and friendly atmosphere where students can relax, socialize, and enjoy their meals. Our team works hard to ensure cleanliness, variety, and a high standard of service to make your dining experience as enjoyable as possible.
